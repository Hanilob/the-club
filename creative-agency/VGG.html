VGG Book 1

It was a black, dark and… well, dark, night. A green slime-blob-thing was attacking literally everything in the city (because you know, what else do slime-blobs do), when out of nowhere, it was smacked in the face -by someone swinging on a rope off of a tall building. “Freeze! You’re under arrest, you, you… um, slimy thing!” Shouted the man, and he posed awkwardly. All of a sudden he took out a diamond sword. “Come with me!”

“Hau, hau, hau!” Laughed the thing in a very “crumbled-up” voice. “And hoow r’ you goin’ too do dat!” The awkward man rolled his eyes. “I’m Hanilob, the… hero-guy! And I don’t want to fight!

The blob-thingy laughed. “I said I won’t fight, unless I have to!” Said Hanilob. Wait, I should attack him, why didn’t I? Thought Hanilob. You know what, I’m going to atta-his thought was interrupted when the blob thing laughed again, and then swallowed him whole. From the back of the nearest building, Hunter rolled his eyes. He took his bow and shot the slime thing in it’s head, slime exploding everywhere.
